## When to Visit Bodrum? The Short Answer

The best time to swim is **June to September**, while **May to October** is ideal for calm and mild weather. The hottest and busiest months are July and August; the most peaceful periods are spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October). The "best month" depends on what you want: energy, peace, or the warmest sea?

In this guide we cover every month by weather, sea temperature and crowds. At the end you will find a table summarising which month is ideal for which traveller.

## Season Overview

It helps to split Bodrum into four periods:

- **Spring (March-May)**: Green nature, mild weather, uncrowded beaches. The swimming season opens towards the end of the period.
- **Summer (June-August)**: Hot, sunny, lively. The sea is at its warmest and crowds at their peak.
- **Autumn (September-October)**: The sea is still warm, crowds have thinned. The most balanced period for many.
- **Winter (November-February)**: Cool and calm. The swimming season is closed, but the town is peaceful.

## Bodrum Month by Month

### March

Around 14-18°C, with occasional rain. Nature begins to green, almond and plum trees blossom. The sea is still cold (16-17°C) and not suited to swimming. March is lovely for walks, nature and calm, for those wanting a quiet escape.

### April

The weather rises to 16-20°C with more sunny days. Beaches are still uncrowded. The sea, around 17-18°C, is cool for most. April is ideal for cycling, nature walks, the castle and bazaar, with beautiful light for photography.

### May

The peak of spring. The weather is usually mild and sunny at 22-26°C. The sea reaches 19-21°C; cool but swimmable for hardy swimmers, warming noticeably towards the end of the month. Beaches are still calm. May is one of the most enjoyable months for those who want to combine calm with fine weather. Our [May guide to Gündoğan beach](/blog/gundugan-plaji-mayis-ayi-rehberi) is a good example for this period.

### June

Summer begins. The weather is 28-31°C and the sea around 23-24°C, now very pleasant for swimming. The first half is still relatively calm; from the second half, crowds start to grow. June is a sweet balance for those who say "I want a summer holiday but not the heaviest crowds."

### July

One of the hottest and liveliest months of the year. Temperatures reach 32-34°C and the sea is very warm at 25-26°C. Beaches and venues are busy and nightlife is at its peak. Ideal for those wanting energy and fun; tiring for those wary of heat and crowds.

### August

Similar to July, at times even hotter (33-35°C). The sea is at its warmest (26-27°C). It is the busiest period of the year. August is the peak for those who want a full summer holiday, a lively atmosphere and the warmest sea; for those seeking calm, it is the most challenging month.

### September

For many, Bodrum's finest month. The weather is still warm (28-31°C), and the sea, warmed all summer, is at its most comfortable (25-26°C). Yet from mid-month, crowds thin noticeably. September is almost perfect for those who want a warm sea but want to avoid the summer rush.

### October

The flavour of autumn. The weather is mild at 23-27°C with plenty of sunny days. The sea is still warm in the first half (22-24°C) and swimming is possible. Beaches are uncrowded, the town calm. October is a great choice for a peaceful, balanced holiday well suited to sightseeing.

### November

The swimming season closes. The weather drops to around 17-20°C and the chance of rain rises. It is no longer suited to swimming, but nature is still beautiful and the town very calm. November suits those seeking quiet, walks and a peaceful escape.

### December - January - February

Winter months are cool and at times rainy (10-15°C). The sea is cold and the swimming season is closed. Still, Bodrum lives in winter too: visiting the castle and museum, walking along the shore, warm seafood-and-meze tables, and a quite different kind of peace. For those wanting a calm escape entirely away from the crowds, winter is a quiet alternative.

| Expectation | Best months |

| Lively nightlife | July, August |
| Family holiday | early June, September, early October |

## A Season Note for Families

For families with children, early June, September and early October are usually the most comfortable: the sea is warm, the weather is not too hot and beaches are quieter. The intense heat of July-August can be hard on small children.
