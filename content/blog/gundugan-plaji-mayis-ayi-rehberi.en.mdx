## 5 Reasons to Go to Gündoğan Beach in May

May is one of the quietest and most enjoyable periods in Bodrum. In this month, Gündoğan beach offers an atmosphere away from the crowds, clean, and perfect for spending time comfortably with children.

1. **Ideal Water Temperature**: In May the sea water is between 20 and 22°C. Children can swim with ease, and you too can stay in the sea for a long time without getting cold.
2. **No Parking Problem**: Unlike the summer season, finding parking is very easy. Even if you arrive between 09:00 and 10:00 in the morning, you'll find a spot close to the beach.
3. **Affordable Prices**: Apartment and hotel prices are 30–40% more economical than in June–August.
4. **Balanced Air Temperature**: With the weather hovering between 22 and 26°C, it's ideal both on the beach and for taking walks with the children.
5. **Local Atmosphere**: Since there are fewer tourists, the fish restaurants and cafés in the area provide a more personal service.

## 6 Activities You Can Do at Gündoğan Beach

### 1. Early Morning Swimming and Snorkelling

In May the sea is very calm between 08:00 and 09:00 in the morning. As water clarity is high, you can easily see the seabed. We recommend getting snorkelling gear (mask-snorkel-fins). Watching small shoals of fish together with the children is a wonderful experience.

**Practical Note**: You can get snorkelling gear from the sporting goods shops in central Bodrum or Turgutreis. If you've rented an apartment, buy the gear in advance and bring it with you.

### 2. Pedal Boats and SUP (Stand-Up Paddle)

At Gündoğan beach in May, some businesses offer pedal boat and SUP rentals. Since it's not crowded, you can use them comfortably with the children.

- **Pedal Boat**: Ideal for 30–45 minute trips. Children really enjoy pedalling.
- **SUP**: A balance exercise and a fun activity. If you're trying it for the first time, it's easier in the morning hours when there's little wind.

### 3. Seafront Walk and Running

The Gündoğan seafront is 2–3 km long. Between 07:00 and 08:00 in the morning it's perfect for running or walking. Walking on the sand and collecting seashells with the children makes for an enjoyable time.

**Local Tip**: Along the seafront in May, local fishermen mend their nets. The children watch the fishing gear with curiosity and ask questions.

### 4. Picnic and Playing in the Sand

The sand at Gündoğan beach is wide and clean. In May, since the sunbeds aren't completely full yet, you can spread out a picnic mat or beach towel and sit comfortably.

**Game Suggestions with Children**:
- Building sandcastles
- Volleyball (bring a portable net)
- Throwing a frisbee
- Water gun games by the sea

### 5. Lunch at Fish Restaurants

The fish restaurants around Gündoğan beach are calm in May, and the prices are reasonable. There's a wide variety of fresh fish and mezes. Having lunch facing the sea view with the children is an enjoyable activity.

**Suggestions for the Family**:
- Chips and pasta options for children are available everywhere.
- The mezes come in shareable portions, so you can try different flavours.
- At midday (13:00–15:00) the sun is overhead, so ask for a shaded table.

### 6. Watching the Sunset in the Early Evening

Gündoğan faces west, so the evening sun sets over the beach. In May, the sunset time is between 19:30 and 20:00. Sitting on the sand and watching the sun go down with the children leaves lovely memories of the holiday.

## Preparation Checklist for Gündoğan in May

### What You Need to Bring:

- **Sun Protection**: The May sun is deceptive. SPF 50 cream, a hat and sunglasses are a must.
- **Beach Gear**: Towel, beach umbrella (if not provided in the apartment), inflatable air bed, snorkelling gear.
- **Children's Needs**: Sand toys, water guns, inflatable armbands (for small children).
- **Clothing**: Light summer clothes, but 1–2 cardigans or sweatshirts for the cool evenings.
- **First Aid Kit**: Burn cream, insect bite ointment, fever reducer (for children).

### Grocery Shopping:

There are a few markets in central Gündoğan. If you've rented an apartment, do a big shop on the first day:
- Breakfast items (cheese, olives, bread, milk)
- Soft drinks and water (to take to the beach)
- Fruit and vegetables (in season, strawberries and cherries are very fresh)
- Pasta, rice (for quick meals)

## Places to Visit Around Gündoğan

In May, beyond the beach, you can also explore the surrounding area:

1. **Yalıkavak**: 15 minutes from Gündoğan. Yalıkavak Marina and its market are worth a visit.
2. **Türkbükü**: 10 minutes away. You can walk along the coastal road and take a break at the boutique cafés.
3. **Gümüşlük**: 25 minutes away. The ancient harbour ruins and the sunset view are very beautiful.
4. **Central Bodrum**: 30 minutes away. You can visit Bodrum Castle and the Museum of Underwater Archaeology.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the weather like in Gündoğan in May?**  
In May the air temperature is generally between 22 and 26°C. Mornings and evenings are slightly cool, while midday is sunny and warm. The chance of rain is low, but there may be scattered showers on 1–2 days.

**Is it safe to go with children?**  
Gündoğan beach is shallow and free of waves. You can go in safely with children. Still, don't leave small children alone in the sea, and use inflatable armbands.
