## Which Are the Best Areas in Bodrum for Families with Children?

The best areas in Bodrum for families with children are Gündoğan, Ortakent, Bitez and Turgutreis. What these four areas have in common is a shallow, wave-free sea, a calm atmosphere and easy access to daily needs. Which is most suitable for you depends on the level of quiet you want and your holiday style.

On a holiday with children, the questions "is the sea safe, is the beach close, is it quiet at night?" matter more than a hotel's star rating. Below we look at each area through a family's eyes, and at the end we give a list of criteria to help you choose the right one.

## How Do You Tell a Family-Friendly Area?

Five points determine whether an area is suitable for families with children:

1. **The shallowness and calmness of the sea**: Coves that stay shallow for a long distance, where children can swim safely, are ideal.
2. **The walking distance to the beach**: Walking a long way with a pushchair in the midday heat is hard; accommodation close to the beach is a big convenience.
3. **Proximity to a shop and pharmacy**: Easy access to basic services for children's needs is important.
4. **A calm atmosphere**: Areas without heavy nightlife are more peaceful for families whose children go to bed early.
5. **The apartment's equipment**: A kitchen, washing machine and spacious area are the elements that ease a family holiday.

## Gündoğan: A Calm Cove, a Peaceful Holiday

Gündoğan, in the north of the peninsula, is an area loved by families for its wave-free, calm character. The sea is usually still, which makes swimming with small children easy.

**Plus points for families:**

- A shallow, calm sea
- A peaceful atmosphere away from the crowds
- A flat strip suitable for walking along the seafront
- Calm evenings, an ideal pace for families with children

Gündoğan is tailor-made for families who say "let's swim, play in the sand and go to bed early." [You can review the Gündoğan apartment options here.](/bodrum/gundogan-apart-kiralama)

## Ortakent: A Wide Beach, a Family-Friendly Pace

Ortakent (Yahşi beach) is a favourite among families with children for its long, wide sandy beach. The sea stays shallow for a long distance; so even if children move a little away from the shore, their feet reach the bottom.

**Plus points for families:**

- A safe sea that stays shallow for a long distance
- Plenty of room for playing in the sand on the wide beach
- Family-friendly venues along the seafront
- A reasonable distance to central Bodrum

On the wide beach there's plenty of space for children to burn off energy, from building castles to volleyball. [You can take a look at the Ortakent apartment options here.](/bodrum/ortakent-apart-kiralama)

## Bitez: Shallow Water, a Calm Shore

Bitez cove has one of the safest seas in Bodrum for families with children. The water stays shallow for a long time and there is little wave. The palm trees and the walking path along the seafront create a pleasant atmosphere for families.

**Plus points for families:**

- A very long stretch of shallow sea (extra safety for small children)
- A seafront walking path, suitable for strolling with a pushchair
- A calm and green setting
- Proximity to central Bodrum

Bitez is one of the first areas that comes to mind for families who ask "how safe can the sea be for a child?" [You can see the Bitez apartment options here.](/bodrum/bitez-apart-kiralama)

## Turgutreis: A Wide Shore, Practical Living

Turgutreis, in the west of the peninsula, is a practical option for families with its long shoreline and a layout that makes daily life easier. Access to basic services like a shop, pharmacy and bakery is easy; its weekly market is also handy for shopping.

**Plus points for families:**

- A long and wide shore
- Easy access to daily needs
- Beautiful sunsets thanks to its west-facing position
- A seafront strip suitable for an evening family walk

For families who value both the sea and a practical way of living on holiday, Turgutreis is a balanced choice. [You can reach the Turgutreis apartment options here.](/bodrum/turgutreis-apart-kiralama)

## Which Area Is Right for Your Family?

- **The calmest, most peaceful holiday**: Gündoğan
- **A wide beach and plenty of play space**: Ortakent
- **The shallowest, safest sea**: Bitez
- **Practical living and a long shore**: Turgutreis

None is "better than the others"; the right area depends on your family's priorities. If you have a baby, Bitez's long shallows may stand out; if you have older children, Ortakent's wide beach may.

## Apartment Selection Criteria for Families with Children

After choosing the area, pay attention to the following in the apartment:

- **Walking distance to the beach**: Within 5–10 minutes if possible.
- **Kitchen equipment**: A stove, fridge and basic kitchenware for preparing children's meals.
- **Washing machine**: On a holiday with children, daily laundry is a big convenience.
- **A spacious and safe area**: A layout where children can play comfortably, with a railed balcony.
- **Air conditioning**: Important for children to sleep comfortably in the summer heat.

## Tips for Safety and Comfort on a Holiday with Children

Once you've chosen the right area and apartment, a few small preparations make the holiday both safe and enjoyable:

- **Protect against the sun**: The Bodrum sun is overhead at midday. High-factor sun cream, a hat and a long-sleeved swim shirt help for children. Take a break in the shade during the hottest hours (12:00–15:00).
- **Sea safety**: Even though the areas are shallow, don't leave small children alone in the sea; use inflatable armbands or a life jacket.
- **Enjoy the morning**: Beaches are both calmer and cooler early in the morning. The morning hours are ideal for going into the sea with children.
- **Use the kitchen advantage**: Thanks to the apartment's kitchen, you can prepare children's meals whenever you like, and check the ingredients yourself for children with allergies.
- **Get to know the immediate area**: On the first day, find out where the nearest pharmacy, shop and health centre are; you'll save time in an emergency.

These small steps make the holiday far more relaxed, especially for families coming to Bodrum for the first time.

## Choosing the Season: When to Go?

The summer season is the hottest and busiest period; even calm coves can get crowded. For families wanting a calmer, cooler holiday with a wider choice, late spring and early autumn are usually more comfortable. In these periods the sea is mild, the beaches are quiet and exploring with children is more enjoyable.

## Conclusion

The best areas in Bodrum for families with children are Gündoğan, Ortakent, Bitez and Turgutreis. They all offer a shallow sea and a calm atmosphere; the choice between them is shaped by your family's priorities.
